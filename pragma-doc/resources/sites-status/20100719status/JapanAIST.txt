> > > (2) Asia GEO Grid Initiative
> > > The proposal for the Ministry of Education, Sports, Culture, 
> > > Science and Technologies entitled "Asia GEO Grid Initiative"
> > > has been accepted and the project started on July 1st, 2010.  
> > > AIST, NECTED, NARL/NCHC, and VAST are participating in this 
> > > project.  The main objective of this joint research is to 
> > > establish a Network of Excellence which promotes and supports 
> > > construction of a cyber infrastructure in Asia for Earth Science.  
> > > In this research, we will (1) hold international workshops and 
> > > forums to provide opportunities for collaboration, (2) support 
> > > researchers in Asian countries to travel for attending the 
> > > workshops and forums, (3) develop Asia GEO Grid Infrastructure 
> > > which federates satellite databases, field sensor databases, and 
> > > simulation programs, etc.  Collaborating with AsiaFlux Network, 
> > > one of expected outcomes is to support and accelerate the 
> > > establishment of a framework and a network for sharing flux data 
> > > in Asia.  This project is tightly related to activities in PRAGMA 
> > > GEO Science Working Group the Asia GEO Grid Infrastructure is 
> > > shared with PRAGMA GEO Science Infrastructure.
> > >
