Hi Cindy,

Thank you for the advanced greeting and also for providing the website links.

Finally, we are going to formally launch the Philippine e-Science Grid. This is long overdue and we are very glad that after so many drawbacks we were finally able to link one of our clusters to other clusters owned by two local universities .  These three clusters form the Philippine e-Science Grid core.

We have several clusters that are operational right now.  Next goal is to connect these machines into one virtual cluster, and finally to connect this virtual machine to PRAGMA Grid.  We plan to do this immediately after the launching.  As much as possible, we don't want to mess up the setup of our clusters now since we will show a lot of demos during the launching.  We are also looking forward to working with you on VM.  We are hoping that we could accommplish something significant on this aspect before the next PRAGMA meeting.
