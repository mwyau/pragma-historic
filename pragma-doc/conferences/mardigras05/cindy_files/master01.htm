How many people knows the BLAST?  It’s should be a very famous application in biology.
Think about the question … if you have a very large DNA sequence composed by four characters, say A, G, C, and T.  and you have no any other information about this DNA. How will you do to identify the genes on it?  The solution here is very simple.  The researchers can do a lot of experiments to sequence a lot of the known gene fragments from cells.  These fragments are collected to be a EST database.  Then they do the alignment between the DNA sequence and the EST database.  and BLAST is the tool to give us the help!!
