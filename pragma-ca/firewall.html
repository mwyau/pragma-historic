Firewall Settings

  • If your site policy allow, it's best to open the ports listed below to the world.

  • If your site policy does not allow to open some of the ports to the world, then open them to
    • all pragma grid clusters. (See the list of hostnames at http://goc.pragma-grid.net/pragma-doc/computegrid.html and http://goc.pragma-grid.net/pragma-doc/cloud.html, just below the resources tables.)
    • other services and portals:
      • Gfarm metaserver: gfarm-meta1.rocksclusters.org (rocks-96.sdsc.edu, 198.202.88.96)
      • Nimrod portals: 130.194.70.13, 130.194.70.14, 130.194.70.17, 130.194.245.102, 130.194.245.103
      • PRAGMA portal: portal.pragma-grid.net (198.202.88.144)
      • PRAGMA Grid Operation server: goc.pragma-grid.net
    Note that in such case, as pragma grid grow and change, you will need to update your firewall settings from time to time. 
Here are the ports required:
----------------------------
http - TCP, default 80
https - TCP, default 443
ldap - TCP, default 389
gfarm - TCP and UDP, 600 and 601
Openssh - TCP, default 22
Iperf - TCP, default 5001 to 198.202.88.96
gatekeeper - TCP, default 2119 and port# 40000-41000
gridftp - TCP and UDP, default 2811 and 2812 and port# 40000-41000
OsakaU Opal service - TCP and UDP, port# 8080-9000
